What is CCSantè Community Conference?

CCSantè - Creative Coding Santè - is a monthly meetup that has been run in person in Bangalore, as well as online for the last 3 years. A group of us started this as a way to create a community around creative coding where creators can share their work, folks can learn, and those who are simply curious can comfortably engage. We have also organised 2 larger events - Processing Community Day - in the past.

You can read about the previous events here

  • Processing Community Day 2020
  • Processing Community Day 2019

CCSante Community Conference is a day to celebrate and explore art, code, and how all these worlds come together. We define the format of our event in any way that serves our community. What does that mean? - aside from the conventional lecture / demo format, the conference will have show & tell sessions, zine-making sessions, an audio-visual performance, and group discussions on software art!.
